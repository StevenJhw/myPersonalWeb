Project

DiDi Monthly Pay

Led the risk assessment team on delivering a new credit product to predict user default rate, through collaborating with cross-functional partners. The new product resulted in a 20% reduction in the default rate while maintaining the same approval rate.

Developed and delivered machine learning models (decision trees, XGBoost, and random forest) to optimize risk modeling strategies. Achieved a 10% increase in approval rate on fixed default rates in offline evaluation, and 7% improvement on monthly average portfolio growth in online AB test.
